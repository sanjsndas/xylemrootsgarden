📜 Privacy Policy
Your data, rooted in respect.

At Xylem Roots Garden, we believe that transparency and ecological ethics extend beyond the soil into the digital realm. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website, services, and community — always honoring your digital sovereignty.

Effective date: April 21, 2026  |  Last revised: April 21, 2026 🌿 GDPR & CCPA aware

1. Who we are & our commitment

Xylem Roots Garden (referred to as "we", "us", "our", or "Xylem Roots") is a gardening education platform, sanctuary, and community hub dedicated to regenerative horticulture. Our website address is: https://xylemroots.garden (and associated subdomains). We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy — just as we nurture microbial life in healthy soil, we protect your personal data from exploitation.

2. Information we may collect

We only collect information that helps us cultivate a better experience for our gardening community. This includes:

  • 🌱 Personal identification data: name, email address, phone number (when voluntarily provided via contact forms, workshop registrations, or consultation requests).
  • 📬 Communication data: messages you send to us, including gardening questions, feedback, or collaboration proposals.
  • 🍃 Technical data: IP address, browser type, approximate geolocation (region/city level), referral URLs, and pages visited — collected through standard server logs and analytics tools to improve our content performance.
  • 🌾 Cookies & similar technologies: see our Cookies Policy for details. You can manage cookie preferences anytime.
🌻 “Minimal & Mindful” principle: We never collect sensitive personal data (like financial or health details) unless explicitly provided for a consultation. We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Ever. That’s a root promise.

3. How we use your information

Every piece of data serves a clear purpose rooted in our mission:

  • ✅ Respond to inquiries: answer your questions about gardening, workshops, or collaborations.
  • ✅ Service improvement: analyze aggregated trends to enhance our 78k+ word compendium and user experience.
  • ✅ Workshop & event coordination: send confirmations, reminders, and follow-up resources (only when you opt-in).
  • ✅ Newsletter & educational content: if you subscribe separately, we’ll send seasonal planting guides, black-theme inspirations, and exclusive insights (unsubscribe link always provided).
  • ✅ Legal compliance & security: protect against fraud, enforce our terms, and comply with applicable laws.

We process data based on legitimate interest, contractual necessity (when you request services), or your explicit consent (e.g., marketing emails).

4. Sharing information – Transparency in symbiosis

We never share your data with third parties except in these limited circumstances:

  • 🔧 Service providers: trusted partners who help operate our website (e.g., secure hosting, email delivery, analytics). They are contractually bound to confidentiality and cannot use your data for any other purpose.
  • ⚖️ Legal obligations: if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation to protect our rights or safety of our community.
  • 🌿 Business transfers: in the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, your data would be transferred with the same privacy commitments.

We do NOT integrate with ad networks, data brokers, or third-party marketing platforms that harvest personal data. Your journey remains undisturbed.

5. Data retention – let your digital roots rest

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. For example:

  • Contact form submissions: kept for 24 months to ensure follow-up continuity, then anonymized.
  • Workshop registration data: retained for 3 years for reporting and future event invitations (with your consent).
  • Analytics logs: aggregated and stripped of identifiers after 14 months.

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

6. Cookies & tracking technologies – gentle digital pollinators

Our website uses essential cookies to function (e.g., remembering form preferences) and optional analytical cookies to understand which gardening articles resonate most. We do not use targeting/advertising cookies. You can adjust your cookie preferences via our cookie banner or browser settings. For full details, please read our separate Cookies Policy.

7. Where we store your data & international transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you are accessing our site from the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, Switzerland, or other regions with different data protection laws, please note that your information may be transferred to the US. We ensure adequate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and maintain a level of protection consistent with GDPR requirements. By using our site, you consent to this transfer, subject to applicable laws.

8. Your privacy rights – you hold the watering can

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in Europe, CCPA in California), you may have the following rights:

  • 🔍 Right to access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • ✏️ Right to rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • 🧹 Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”): request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • ⛔ Right to restrict processing: limit how we use your data.
  • 📤 Right to data portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • 🚫 Right to object: opt-out of direct marketing or processing based on legitimate interests.
  • 🍪 Cookie consent withdrawal: manage preferences via our cookie settings.

To exercise any right, please contact us at privacy@xylemroots.garden or via our contact form. We will respond within 30 days. No fee will be charged unless requests are manifestly unfounded or excessive.

🌟 California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt-out of any “sale” of data. We do not sell your personal information. For any requests, email privacy@xylemroots.garden with “CCPA Request” in the subject line.

9. Children’s privacy – tender shoots

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

10. Third-party links & embedded content

Our articles may include links to external gardening resources, seed suppliers, or educational videos. These third-party sites have separate privacy policies, and we hold no responsibility for their practices. We encourage you to read their policies before engaging. Embedded content (e.g., YouTube videos) behaves as if you visited the source site, which may collect data about your interaction.

11. Data security – we build digital raised beds

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data: SSL encryption (HTTPS), firewalls, access controls, regular security audits, and staff training on data privacy. While no system is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information. In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.

12. Changes to this privacy policy

As our garden evolves, we may update this policy to reflect new practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes via a prominent notice on this page or email (if you have provided one). The “last revised” date at the top indicates when the policy was last updated. Your continued use of the website after changes signifies acceptance.

13. Contact our Data Protection Steward

If you have any questions, concerns, or would like to report a privacy issue, please reach out to our dedicated Data Protection Steward (aka Head of Digital Ecology):

  • 📧 Email: privacy@xylemroots.garden
  • 📬 Mail: Xylem Roots Garden – Attn: Privacy Office, 1420 Evergreen Terrace, Garden District, Portland, OR 97214, USA
  • 🌐 Web form: Contact Us page (choose “Privacy inquiry”)

For EU residents, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

🌱 This privacy policy is a living document, rooted in accountability and reverence for your digital footprint. Thank you for trusting Xylem Roots Garden — where every interaction is cultivated with care.
